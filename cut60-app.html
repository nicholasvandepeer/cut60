01
Days 1-30 / 24 Mar - 22 Apr
Establish the cut
Hit the calorie and protein targets consistently every day. Build the routine. Two runs per week. Weights at current working weights - do not go lighter. Expect 2-2.5 kg of fat loss. The first week will show a sharper drop from water and glycogen which is normal.

02
Days 31-60 / 23 Apr - 22 May
Push and consolidate
Add the Sunday easy run. Extend Wednesday run to 45-50 min. Increase Saturday HIIT to 10 intervals. If weight loss has stalled by day 30, reduce rest day calories by 100 kcal only. Do not slash carbs on training days - you need them to keep lifting heavy.

After day 60

If you hit 82 kg
Take a diet break
2 weeks at maintenance (~2,980 kcal) to reset hormones and hunger signals. Then continue cutting toward 78 kg with a further 8-10 week cut at the same deficit.
Supplements
On a deficit these matter more
Creatine 5g daily. Vitamin D3 3,000 IU. Omega-3 fish oil 2-3g EPA/DHA. Whey protein only if struggling to hit 194g from food.
